Legal Warning & Protections

MiWITNESS.org is an independent, non-profit public interest project. Our mission is to assist, empower, and protect innocent victims of systemic violations, and to support research and investigations in the public good.

Legal Protections

  • Any interference, obstruction, intimidation, or retaliation against MiWITNESS, its users, or its complaint process by authorities, organizations, or individuals is strictly prohibited and may be unlawful under UK and international law.
  • All communications, complaints, and evidence generated through MiWITNESS are protected as public interest disclosures and may be used for legal proceedings and research.
  • Unauthorized access, tampering with digital evidence, or attempts to suppress or intimidate users will trigger immediate legal procedures and public reporting.
  • Any actions taken by authorities or organizations to interfere with MiWITNESS or its users will be documented, reported, and challenged as unlawful.

Your Rights & Support

  • If you experience interference, threats, or intimidation as a result of using MiWITNESS, contact info@miwitness.org for support and guidance.
  • MiWITNESS is committed to your protection. We will take all necessary steps to safeguard your rights and report violations to the proper legal and oversight bodies.
  • All users are encouraged to retain copies of their complaints and correspondence as digital evidence.

Public Interest Statement

MiWITNESS operates solely for research, public interest, and the protection of the innocent. All content, tools, and communications are provided independently and are not subject to control by any external authority or organization.
