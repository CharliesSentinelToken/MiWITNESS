About MiWITNESS

MiWITNESS is a public interest, independent digital advocate for victims of systemic injustice. We use technology, research, and the power of AI to help individuals understand their rights, document violations, and generate complaints securely and privately.

Our Mission

  • Empower innocent victims with plain-English legal tools.
  • Expose and document systemic failures and abuses.
  • Remain independent, non-profit, and free from outside control.
  • Operate transparently, with no data collection or tracking.
