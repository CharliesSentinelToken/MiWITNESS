Privacy Notice

MiWITNESS.org is committed to your privacy, safety, and transparency in all our advocacy and research work.

No Data Collection

  • We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data or identifying information about our users.

  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

Public Interest & Research Only

  • MiWITNESS is a non-profit, public-interest project. No ownership or proprietary claim is made over any data, advice, or content generated for research or advocacy purposes.
  • All advice, legal information, and assistance are provided strictly as research and for the public good.

External Services

  • AI responses are generated via OpenAI and processed through GitHub Actions, without storing your queries or results.
  • Email delivery is handled by EmailJS; we recommend reviewing their privacy policy to understand their data practices.

Your Rights

  • You are always in control of your information. If you have concerns or wish to learn more, please contact us at info@miwitness.org.
